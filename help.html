Welcome to the Join Help Page

Your go-to guide for using our Kanban Board Project Management Tool.

Join is Kanban-based Project Management Tool which can effectively visualise your project information.

It's important to note that Join is designed as an educational exercise and is not intended for extensive business use. Consistent availability, reliability, accuracy, or other aspects of quality regarding Join cannot be guaranteed.

Getting Started

  • Login: Access your account by logging in with your credentials, use the guest login or sign up for a new user account.
  • Dashboard Overview: After login, explore the dashboard to familiarize yourself with various features.

Contacts Page:

  • Add New Contacts: Click on 'New Contact' to add details relevant to your contact.
  • View Contacts: All your contacts are listed here for easy reference.

Board Page:

  • Task Management: View and manage tasks categorized as 'To Do', 'In Progress', 'Await Feedback', and 'Done'.
  • Drag & Drop: Easily move tasks between categories by dragging and dropping them.

Add Task Page:

  • Create New Task: Enter task details and click 'Create Task'. Your task will appear in the 'To Do' section of the board.

Summary Page

  • Get a quick overview of total task number.
  • Most urgent task and it's due date.
  • Count of tasks in 'To Do' and 'Done' categories.
  • Current logged in user.
